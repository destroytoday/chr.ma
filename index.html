Catalog interesting things

Chroma is a simple, powerful app for identifying objects around you. It’s a fun way to catalog curious things and share them with friends.

Figures

Capture an object from every angle

Share a series of images so friends can get the full view.

Fields

Categorize it

Pick a category so people can find your figures, or create a new one to fit your needs.

Add details

Each category has a standard set of fields to describe your figure. Add identifying details like materials, dimensions, names, and more.

Custom fields

Add custom fields to define new attributes. You can also ask people to help you fill in the details.

Streams

Find people who share your interests

Follow people and categories

Create a stream by following people and categories that interest you.

Swipe to switch streams

Swipe left or right to switch to a different stream.

Post anywhere

Share figures to Facebook, Twitter, or with your contacts.

  • Twitter
  • Email

Maps

Put your discoveries on the map

Add a location to any figure so people can see where you found it.
